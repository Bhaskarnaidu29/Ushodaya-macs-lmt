How Advance Works
What is Advance Deposit?

When a member pays in advance, the amount is stored and automatically applied during their next recovery posting (EMI collection).

Application Priority:

1 Arrear Principal
2 Arrear Interest
3 Current Principal Due
4 Current Interest Due
5 Savings Due
6 Remaining → Additional Savings
Advance is automatically applied during the next recovery posting for this loan.
Important Notes
  • Advance amount is stored in AdvanceRecovery table
  • Not applied to LoanRec immediately
  • Auto-applied during next week's recovery posting
  • Balance can be checked anytime
  • Multiple advances can be posted for same loan
